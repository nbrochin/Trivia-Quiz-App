How to Play:

Some Things to Keep in Mind for the Quiz Buzz Trivia Game:


  1. 1. Press the new game button in the upper right hand corner to start.
  2. 2. You can also choose a topic scrolling by in the header to start a game.
  3. 3. All questions are multiple choice. Make an answer selection and then press the "next question" button to score your quiz and move on.
  4. 4. Each question is worth between 5 and 25 points depending on level of difficulty.
  5. 5. The colored dots will indicate whether you got the previous question right or wrong: green indicates a correct answer, while red indicates a wrong one.
  6. 6. Some quiz topics are harder than others and have more 25pt questions in them.
  7. 7. Check the high score button in the footer (lower left) to see what the score to beat is for all quizzes.

Good luck, and be smart!
