How to Play:

Here are the rules for the Quiz Buzz Trivia Game:


  1. 1. Press the new game button in the upper right hand corner to begin a game.
  2. 2. A question will appear, shortly followed by four possible answers.
  3. 3. Each question is worth between 5 and 25 points depending on its level of difficulty. The colored dots will indicate how difficult a question is: green is the easiest, followed by blue, orange red and then black.
  4. 4. You will have 10 seconds to answer each question, after which the next question will appear.

So, are you ready? Good luck!
